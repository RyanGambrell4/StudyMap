Framework recall

Finance and accounting drills

Why business students need a multi-mode study tool

A typical business semester is one quantitative course (finance, accounting, stats), one case-based course (strategy, marketing), one writing-heavy course, and a group-project course. Different modes, different methods, different planning. Most study apps were built for one mode. StudyEdge AI handles all four with course-specific session blueprints and a schedule that respects the differences.

How a typical business students week looks in StudyEdge AI

You add your courses, exam dates, and the topics you are weakest on. The system generates a weekly schedule with specific sessions: which course, which topic, what method, how long. Active recall runs in-session. After each session you log how confident you feel; the next week's schedule reweights toward shaky topics and lightens up on solid ones.
