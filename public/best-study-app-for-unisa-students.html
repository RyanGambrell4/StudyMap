For UNISA Students

UNISA gives you the qualification. You have to supply the structure yourself. StudyEdge AI helps.

Distance learning works. But it requires self-discipline, consistent effort, and a system for organising content you've never heard explained out loud. StudyEdge AI is that system.

The study plan UNISA doesn't give you

UNISA provides study guides, assignment dates, and an examination timetable. It does not tell you how to allocate your study hours across multiple modules or how to pace your preparation across a semester. StudyEdge AI builds that plan, reverse-engineered from your exam dates and calibrated to your available hours each week.

An AI tutor when you're stuck on the study guide

UNISA study guides are thorough but dense. When you read a section and it does not click, there is no lecturer to ask. StudyEdge AI's AI tutor explains concepts in plain language, provides alternative examples, and answers follow-up questions until the material makes sense. It is available at any hour, on any device.

Active recall instead of passive re-reading

Most UNISA students study by reading and re-reading their study guide. This feels like studying but produces weak retention. StudyEdge AI runs active recall sessions: you produce answers from memory, then check them. This is what actually prepares you for UNISA's closed-book written examinations.

Scheduling around full-time work

Many UNISA students are full-time employees completing a degree alongside work. You enter your actual available hours each day, whether that is 6am before your shift, your lunch break, or two hours on Sunday morning. StudyEdge AI builds the most effective study plan that fits those constraints.

Assignment and exam mark tracking

UNISA's final mark combines assignment marks and written exam performance. Grade Hub tracks your assignment marks and weights as results arrive, showing your current semester mark and what you need on the written exam to pass or achieve a distinction. You know your target before you walk into the exam hall.

Works on mobile data in low-connectivity areas

UNISA students are spread across South Africa, including areas where wifi is unreliable. StudyEdge AI is designed to be lightweight in the browser, and the iOS app caches your study plan and flashcards locally. You can study effectively on mobile data or offline after loading your session once.
