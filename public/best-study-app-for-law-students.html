Why law students need an integrated planner

Law school grades depend almost entirely on a single final per course. The build-up is reading, briefing, outlining, hypos. Most study apps do not understand this structure. StudyEdge AI builds the schedule around it: reading volume per week, outline-building blocks every week, hypo practice in the last month. The plan adapts when you fall behind on reading, instead of pretending you can catch up by cramming finals week.

How a typical law students week looks in StudyEdge AI

You add your courses, exam dates, and the topics you are weakest on. The system generates a weekly schedule with specific sessions: which course, which topic, what method, how long. Active recall runs in-session. After each session you log how confident you feel; the next week's schedule reweights toward shaky topics and lightens up on solid ones.
