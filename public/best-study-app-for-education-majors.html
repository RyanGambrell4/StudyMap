Why education majors need a different study system

Education programs are unusual in that the academic workload competes directly with clinical time. Field placements, student teaching hours, and observation requirements are not optional, and they are scheduled by someone else. Your study system needs to work around a calendar you do not fully control. StudyEdge AI builds sessions around your actual available blocks, not a hypothetical week where you have four free hours every afternoon.

How Praxis prep fits into the system

Most education students treat Praxis prep as a separate task from their coursework, which means it either gets neglected or it competes with exam preparation. StudyEdge AI integrates Praxis prep as one item in your overall schedule, allocating weekly session time based on your test date relative to your course exams. You do not have to manage two separate systems.
