What It Does

Built for how math is actually learned and tested

Six features that address the specific ways math students fall behind and fail to recover.

Timed problem set sessions

Math exams are timed. Practice should be too. Each session includes problem sets run under the same conditions, so the exam environment does not catch you off guard.

Concept-level explanations

The AI Tutor does not just show the next step. It explains why the approach works, what theorem or rule applies, and what the common wrong turn looks like at each stage.

Weakness targeting

After each session, you mark which topics felt shaky. The next week's schedule automatically weights more time toward those topics and eases up on what you have mastered.

Multi-course load balancing

Math students often carry two or three quantitative courses simultaneously. The planner allocates hours by exam proximity across all courses so nothing falls through.

Grade Hub with target tracking

Log each quiz and exam score. Grade Hub shows your current standing, what you need on remaining assignments to hit your target grade, and which assignments carry the most weight.

Proof and derivation practice

For upper-division courses, the AI generates proof-based problems and can walk through formal proofs step by step, covering strategy selection before the line-by-line mechanics.

Why passive review fails math students

The single most common mistake math students make is treating the subject like a reading subject. You cannot study math by re-reading the textbook, watching solution videos, or reviewing worked examples. You can only study math by doing math under conditions that simulate the exam. StudyEdge AI builds sessions around that principle.

How a math student's week looks in StudyEdge AI

You add your courses, exam dates, and the chapters covered on each exam. The app generates a weekly schedule broken into sessions: which topic, which type of problem, how long. Active recall is built into every session. After each one you rate your confidence on each topic, and the schedule updates automatically for the following week.
