Frequently asked questions

How do I calculate what grade I need on my final?

Enter your grades so far, their weights, and your target grade. StudyEdge calculates what you need on every remaining assignment and shows three scenarios: minimum needed, current pace, and best case.

Is it free?

Yes. Free for one course, no credit card required. Pro ($12.99/month) unlocks grade tracking for up to 5 courses.

How is this different from RogerHub?

RogerHub does the math. StudyEdge does the math and builds you a study plan to actually hit the grade. When it shows you need an 88 on your final, it adjusts your schedule to help you get there.

Can it track my grades all semester?

Yes. Log every assignment as you go. Your projected grade and what-you-need calculations update in real time all semester.

Does it work with weighted grades?

Yes. Set custom weights for each assignment category (exams, homework, quizzes, participation) and the calculator accounts for them in all projections.
