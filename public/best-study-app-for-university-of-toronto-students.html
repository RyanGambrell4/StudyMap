For UofT Students

UofT is hard. The bell curve makes it harder. Preparation is the only lever you control.

At UofT, your relative performance matters as much as your absolute score. The students who do well have not just studied harder, they have studied smarter. StudyEdge AI builds the system that produces that kind of preparation.

Bell curve preparation

In courses where grades are curved, the students who perform at the top have done deep practice, not surface review. StudyEdge AI generates practice problems at exam difficulty, runs timed retrieval sessions, and identifies the gaps in your understanding before the exam does. Pre-med GPA protection

EngSci at UofT is considered one of the most demanding undergraduate engineering programmes in Canada. The first two years cover maths, physics, chemistry, computer science, and circuit theory simultaneously. StudyEdge AI builds your week around all these courses, and the AI tutor handles the problem-solving load with step-by-step explanations you can ask follow-up questions about.

College culture and academic planning

Whether you're at Trinity, Victoria, St. Michael's, or New College, UofT's federated college system means your social environment and academic support differs by college, but your grade pressure is the same across the faculty. StudyEdge AI focuses on the academics: your courses, your exams, your GPA trajectory.

Rotman Commerce and graduate school preparation

Rotman Commerce students aiming for top MBA programmes or finance careers face a highly competitive academic cohort. StudyEdge AI tracks GPA by semester and provides practice in accounting, finance, statistics, and economics, the core courses where your grade in first and second year shapes what programmes you can access later.
