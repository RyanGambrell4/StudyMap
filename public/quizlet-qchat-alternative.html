Common questions about switching from Q-Chat

Why did Quizlet shut down Q-Chat?
Quizlet shut down Q-Chat in June 2025, citing accuracy issues and concerns about students using AI tutoring for academic dishonesty. The feature was expensive to maintain and Quizlet ultimately decided the risks weren't worth it for their platform.
Can I import my Quizlet decks into StudyEdge AI?
There's no direct Quizlet import, but StudyEdge AI generates new, better-organized flashcard sets from your course topics in seconds. You won't miss the old decks — the AI creates cards calibrated specifically to what you're studying, not generic content from other students' decks.
Is it free like Q-Chat was?
Yes — there's a free plan with no credit card required. It includes AI flashcard generation, basic quizzes, and study planning for up to two courses. Pro (from $2.99/week) adds unlimited courses, the AI Study Coach, practice exams, spaced repetition, and grade tracking. A 7-day free trial of Pro is available.
Does it work on mobile?
Yes — the app is fully mobile-optimized and works on any phone browser. Add it to your home screen on iOS or Android for a near-native app experience. A dedicated mobile app is on the roadmap.
