Engineering workload management

Commerce and law at NWU

The NWU School of Business and Governance and the Faculty of Law deliver commerce, accounting, and LLB programmes. StudyEdge AI generates practice problems, case studies, and hypotheticals for financial accounting, taxation, and legal reasoning, with step-by-step explanations from the AI tutor.

Mafikeng campus student support

Students at the Mafikeng campus often have fewer on-campus support resources than Potchefstroom. StudyEdge AI provides the AI tutor, flashcard generation, and study planning that would otherwise require a study group or face-to-face tutoring. NSFAS grade tracking

NWU has a significant NSFAS student population. Grade Hub tracks your running mark in each module so you can see early whether a course is trending toward the minimum pass required to retain your bursary. This early warning is more useful than discovering the problem after results are released.

Load-shedding resilience

All three NWU campuses are affected by load-shedding. StudyEdge AI's iOS app caches your study plan and flashcards locally. The browser version also loads your last session from cache. Plan your intensive study for before a scheduled cut, and keep going through the outage with cached content.
