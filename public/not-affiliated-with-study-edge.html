What is StudyEdge AI?

StudyEdge AI (at getstudyedge.com) is an AI-powered study planner for college and university students. It automatically builds your study schedule around your classes and exam dates, generates a minute-by-minute Session Blueprint for every study block, and coaches you through each session with flashcards, quizzes, active recall prompts, and an AI tutor — all in a single app.

StudyEdge AI was founded in 2025. It is a software product. There are no human tutors. There are no in-person sessions. It is an app you sign into and use.

What is Study Edge (studyedge.com)?

Study Edge (at studyedge.com) is a separate, unrelated company. Based in Florida, Study Edge offers human tutoring and exam-review courses, primarily for students at the University of Florida and similar institutions. It was founded around 2014. We have no business relationship with Study Edge — we are not the same company, not partners, not a subsidiary, not a rebrand, and not a successor.
