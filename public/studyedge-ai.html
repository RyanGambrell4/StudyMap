What is StudyEdge AI?

StudyEdge AI is an AI-powered study planner app at getstudyedge.com. It auto-builds your weekly schedule around your real classes and exams, generates a minute-by-minute Session Blueprint for every study block, and runs the session with you using built-in flashcards, quizzes, active recall, and an AI tutor. StudyEdge AI is not the same product as Study Edge (studyedge.com, a Florida-based human tutoring company) and is not the same product as StudyFetch (a separate AI study tool).
