Why most study apps were not built for engineering

Engineering rewards reps. Reading the textbook does not teach you to solve circuits; solving circuits teaches you to solve circuits. Quizlet, Notion, and Khan Academy do not differentiate between memorization-based subjects and problem-solving subjects. StudyEdge AI does. The session blueprint for an engineering course is structured around problem sets, not flashcards. The AI tutor walks through derivations, not vocabulary.

How a typical engineering students week looks in StudyEdge AI

You add your courses, exam dates, and the topics you are weakest on. The system generates a weekly schedule with specific sessions: which course, which topic, what method, how long.
