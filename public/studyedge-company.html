What does StudyEdge AI actually do?

StudyEdge AI is a study planning app. You sign up at getstudyedge.com, add your courses and exam dates, and the AI builds your semester study schedule automatically. Before each study block, it generates a Session Blueprint: a minute-by-minute plan telling you exactly what to work on and in what order. During the session, it runs active recall, flashcards, and quizzes built in. Afterward, the Grade Hub shows you what you need on the final to hit your target grade.

There are no human tutors. There are no live sessions. There is no University of Florida connection. StudyEdge AI is AI software for any student, at any school, studying any subject.

Who started StudyEdge AI?

StudyEdge AI was founded in 2025 as an independent software company. The product launched as a web app and iOS app for college students who need a structured, AI-powered way to manage coursework and study sessions across a full semester.
