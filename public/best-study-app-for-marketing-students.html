Why marketing students benefit from application-focused study sessions

Marketing is often seen as a softer business major, which makes students underestimate how much preparation its exams actually require. Consumer behavior courses in particular draw on psychology research that demands both conceptual understanding and the ability to apply theories like the Elaboration Likelihood Model or the Theory of Planned Behavior to real case scenarios. Students who only review slides and highlight readings show up to exams recognizing concepts they cannot actually use. StudyEdge AI trains application from the first study session, so recognition becomes genuine analytical fluency by exam day.
