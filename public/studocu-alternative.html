Why Students Switch

A document library is not a study system

Studocu solves the problem of finding study material on the internet. It does not solve the problem of actually using it to prepare for an exam.

Course-specific content, generated fresh

The AI knows what course you are in and what topic is up next. It generates flashcards, summaries, and practice problems calibrated to that material, instead of returning whatever someone uploaded from a different professor's class three years ago.

A real study schedule

Add your exam dates. The system builds a weekly plan with specific sessions: what to study, how long, and which topic, prioritized by what is coming up next. Studocu does not know when your exams are.

Active recall every session

Reading a study guide is passive. StudyEdge AI builds in recall prompts, brain dumps, and self-testing, the methods proven to produce strong retention.

An AI tutor with context

Stuck on a concept? Ask the tutor. It knows what course you are in, can re-explain in different ways, generate similar examples, and quiz you on the underlying idea until you have it.

Grade Hub

Enter your grades; the system tells you what you need on remaining work to land your target GPA. The study plan adjusts to allocate hours toward the courses with the most grade leverage.

Adaptive planning

Bombed a quiz? The schedule reweights toward that topic. Crushed a midterm? Hours redistribute to where they are needed more. The plan is a living document, not a static PDF.
