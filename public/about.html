What we fix

Three problems every student has. One system that fixes all of them.

StudyEdge AI was built around the gap between knowing you should study and actually executing a real plan.

01

No real schedule

Most students start the term with five apps and a vague plan. StudyEdge AI maps your courses, exam dates, and free time into one schedule. It picks the right course at the right time, prioritized by what is actually due next.

02

No session plan

Sitting down to study without a plan wastes the first twenty minutes every time. StudyEdge AI writes a minute-by-minute blueprint for every session: warm-up recall, deep dive, practice, review. You just press start.

03

Passive studying

Re-reading notes feels productive and barely works. StudyEdge AI runs the session live with active recall prompts, flashcards, and quick quizzes built into the timer. Your brain is forced to retrieve, not just review.
