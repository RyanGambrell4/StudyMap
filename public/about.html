What is StudyEdge?

StudyEdge is an AI-powered study app built for college and university students. Most students juggle five different apps to get through a semester — a planner, a flashcard app, a quiz tool, a grade calculator, and something to actually manage their time. StudyEdge replaces all of them.

You add your courses and exam dates. StudyEdge builds your study schedule around your real life, plans every session with a minute-by-minute AI blueprint, runs the session with a structured timer, and tracks your grades throughout the semester. Everything feeds into the same system — your schedule, your sessions, your study tools, and your grades.

StudyEdge was built by students who were frustrated by the same problem: there were plenty of individual study tools, but nothing that put them together into a coherent system. StudyEdge is that system.

What StudyEdge includes

AI Study Schedule

StudyEdge builds your weekly study schedule automatically based on your courses, exam dates, and free time. Syncs to Google Calendar.

Session Blueprint

Before every session, StudyEdge AI builds a minute-by-minute plan for that specific course and topic. Warm-up, review, recall, quiz, synthesis.

Focus Mode Timer

A structured session timer that runs each study block — no willpower required. StudyEdge keeps you on track from start to finish.

AI Study Coach

For each course, StudyEdge's Study Coach builds a multi-week plan, identifies weak areas, and adapts as you progress.

AI Tutor

Ask StudyEdge anything from your courses. The AI Tutor gives course-specific answers and flags difficult topics in your study plan.

AI Flashcards

StudyEdge generates flashcards from any topic, your notes, or uploaded PDFs — directly inside your study session.

Practice Quizzes

AI-generated multiple-choice quizzes with answer explanations, built into every StudyEdge study session.

Active Recall

Evidence-based recall prompts built into every session. StudyEdge makes active recall automatic — not something you have to remember to do.

Grade Hub

StudyEdge tracks your grades all semester and shows your projected final grade. It tells you exactly what you need on remaining assignments to hit your target.
