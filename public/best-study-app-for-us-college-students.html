UCLA

UCLA runs on a 10-week quarter system, which compresses a full semester of content into less time. Pre-med competition is fierce, the UC GPA scale awards honors points, and STEM programs move fast. Built for Bruins who cannot afford to fall behind.

NYU

Stern Business and Tandon Engineering students face intense academic pressure in one of the world's most distracting cities. High tuition raises the stakes on every grade. For NYU students who need to protect their GPA and stay focused in New York.

University of Michigan

Ross Business School and Michigan Engineering attract some of the most academically competitive undergrads in the country. Big Ten academic culture means rigorous coursework across all programs. For Michigan students competing at the top of a high-achieving cohort.

UT Austin

McCombs Business and Cockrell Engineering have selective internal admissions that require maintaining strong GPAs. Texas automatic admission students arrive needing to prove they belong at a top 50 university. For Longhorns who need to hold their standing in large, competitive courses.

Georgia Tech

One of the hardest engineering schools in the country, known for grade deflation, a CS and engineering double major culture, and a co-op system that means students need to stay sharp while working. For Yellow Jackets navigating one of the most demanding technical curricula in the US.

Penn State

Introductory courses at Penn State regularly seat 200 to 400 students. Smeal College of Business and the Schreyer Honors College demand rigorous academic performance. For Nittany Lions managing large-class anonymity and high academic expectations simultaneously.

Ohio State

One of the largest universities in the US by enrollment, Ohio State's Fisher College of Business and pre-med pipeline are among the busiest in the country. For Buckeyes navigating a massive campus and a course catalog that requires real self-direction.

University of Florida

Bright Futures scholars must maintain GPA minimums to keep scholarship funding. Warrington College of Business and a competitive pre-med program create high grade pressure. For Gators who need to protect both their scholarship and their academic record.

Purdue

Purdue Engineering is one of the most respected in the country and one of the most demanding. Grade deflation in core engineering courses is well documented. Krannert Business students face their own rigorous quantitative curriculum. For Boilermakers who need a system that matches the workload.

Texas A&M

Engineering and Agriculture powerhouse with a strong Aggie identity and demanding technical programs. Graduate school pipelines in engineering and veterinary medicine require sustained high GPAs. For Aggies balancing a proud academic culture with a heavy course load.

How It Works

Built for the US college experience

StudyEdge AI is not a generic flashcard app. It is a complete academic performance system designed around how US universities actually work.

4.0 GPA tracking

Grade Hub tracks your GPA on the standard US 4.0 scale. Enter your courses, credit hours, and letter grades. See your semester GPA, cumulative GPA, and what you need in each remaining course to hit your target. Works for semester and quarter systems.

Personalized study schedules

Tell StudyEdge AI your exam dates, assignment deadlines, and how many hours you can study per day. It generates a day-by-day study plan that distributes your preparation intelligently across every course, so nothing gets neglected and nothing gets crammed at the last minute.

Active recall coaching

Passive re-reading is the least effective way to study. StudyEdge AI runs retrieval practice sessions for each subject, generating questions based on your material and drilling the concepts that need the most work. The sessions are timed and scored so you know exactly where your gaps are.

AI subject tutor

Whether you are stuck on organic chemistry mechanisms, matrix algebra, microeconomics theory, or constitutional law, the AI tutor explains concepts from first principles, walks through problems step by step, and answers follow-up questions until it clicks. Available for every subject.

Pre-med and professional program support

Medical school, law school, MBA, and engineering grad programs all require competitive undergraduate GPAs. StudyEdge AI flags when a course is threatening your cumulative average early enough to change course and practice problems at the depth that pre-professional programs demand.

Semester and quarter system support

Whether your school runs on a 15-week semester or a 10-week quarter, StudyEdge AI builds your schedule around your actual academic calendar. Quarter-system students get a plan that accounts for the faster pace. Semester students get fuller ramp-up time before major exams.
