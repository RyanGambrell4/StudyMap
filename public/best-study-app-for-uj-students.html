For UJ Students

UJ students are not studying in ideal conditions. The study plan should reflect that.

Most study apps assume a student who has all afternoon free and reliable wifi at home. UJ students are more often working a shift, commuting two hours, and fitting studying into whatever gaps remain. StudyEdge AI was built around real constraints, not ideal ones.

Scheduling around your real availability

Enter how many hours you actually have each day, factoring in your commute, your part-time job, and your family commitments. StudyEdge AI builds a plan that fits those hours rather than one that assumes you have a full day available every day. Every available hour gets used intelligently.

Study on the commute

UJ students across all four campuses often spend significant time in taxis, buses, or Gautrain coaches. StudyEdge AI's mobile interface is designed to work on any phone, with fast load times on mobile data. You can run flashcard sessions or review your study plan for the day during your commute.

NSFAS progress tracking

NSFAS bursaries require maintaining academic progress. Grade Hub shows your running percentage in each module so you can see early if a course is at risk, not after the results come out. You can adjust your study allocation in time to make a difference.

Applied sciences and engineering preparation

UJ's Faculty of Engineering and Built Environment and Faculty of Science cover applied maths, physics, chemistry, and engineering disciplines. The AI tutor handles problem-solving in all these areas with step-by-step explanations, and the practice question generator gives you unlimited reps on the topics you find hardest.

Business and education module support

The College of Business and Economics and the Faculty of Education are UJ's largest. StudyEdge AI generates practice questions and essay prompts for business management, marketing, accounting, and education modules, and walks through answers in the structured way UJ assessments expect.

Exam-period balance across campuses

UJ students sometimes have exams across different campuses. The exam planner lets you note campus location for each exam and builds a balanced revision schedule that accounts for travel time. You see all your exam dates and study allocations in one view.
