Why passive reading is not studying for English courses

Most English students read all the assigned texts and still do poorly on essay exams. The problem is that reading and studying are not the same thing. Studying English means being able to produce arguments, recall key passages and their significance, and apply critical frameworks without the text in front of you. That requires active recall practice, not more reading.
