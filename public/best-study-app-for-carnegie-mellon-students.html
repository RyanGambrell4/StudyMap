Why CMU students need a better system than most

CMU's reputation for grade deflation is real. In core CS and engineering courses, median grades routinely fall in the B range even for talented students. The courses that trip people up the most — algorithms, systems, theoretical CS — aren't harder because the material is inherently incomprehensible. They're harder because students try to learn them passively, reviewing slides instead of actively testing recall. StudyEdge AI builds study sessions around active recall and spaced review, which is how technical material actually sticks.
