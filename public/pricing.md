# Pricing — StudyEdge AI (getstudyedge.com)

AI study planner for college students. Free to start, no credit card required.

## Free
- Price: $0/month, forever
- Courses: 1 course
- AI actions: 10 AI actions/month
- Included features: 1 free use of Session Blueprints, Focus Mode, and AI Coach; flashcards, quizzes, active recall
- Trial: No credit card required

## Pro
- Weekly price: $2.99/week
- Monthly price: $9.99/month
- Annual price: $69.99/year ($5.83/month equivalent)
- Courses: 5 courses
- AI actions: 100 AI study boosts/month
- Included features: AI Study Coach, Session Blueprints, Focus Mode, AI Flashcard Maker, Google Calendar sync, smart scheduler, Grade Hub, Syllabus import, streak tracking
- Trial: 3-day free trial, cancel anytime

## Unlimited
- Weekly price: $4.99/week
- Monthly price: $14.99/month
- Annual price: $119.99/year ($10.00/month equivalent)
- Courses: Unlimited
- AI actions: Unlimited
- Included features: Everything in Pro, plus AI Tutor with session memory, advanced practice exam analytics, predicted exam score, priority support
- Trial: Cancel anytime

## Notes
- All plans include web app access at getstudyedge.com and iOS app
- Annual plans offer the best per-month rate (save ~40% vs monthly)
- Weekly billing available for students who prefer pay-as-you-go
- Cancel or downgrade at any time from account settings
- For support: support@getstudyedge.com
