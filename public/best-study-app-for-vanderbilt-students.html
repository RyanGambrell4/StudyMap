Why Vanderbilt pre-med students need grade-tracking from day one

Medical schools calculate your GPA to two decimal places. A single bad semester in your BCPM courses can take years to offset with a higher GPA in upper-level science. Vanderbilt students who manage their grade standing continuously — and make real-time adjustments to their study allocation — outperform those who only look at their GPA at semester's end. The earlier you identify a course that needs more attention, the more time you have to fix it.
