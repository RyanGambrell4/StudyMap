Key Takeaways
  • Different subjects require fundamentally different study approaches -- reading-heavy humanities courses reward synthesis; problem-based STEM courses reward execution
  • For any subject, the professor's exam and assignment patterns are the most reliable signal of what and how to study
  • Subject-specific tutoring (office hours, tutoring centers) produces faster improvement than general study strategy changes
  • The first week of a new subject should focus on understanding its structure -- what the course builds toward, how concepts connect
  • StudyEdge AI generates subject-specific quiz content from your own course notes, bridging the gap between general study techniques and your actual material
