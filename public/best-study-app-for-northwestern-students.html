Why Northwestern students need a quarter-system study strategy

Semester-school study habits — slow ramp-up, mid-semester review, late push before finals — are poorly suited to Northwestern's quarter system. With ten weeks instead of sixteen, every phase is compressed: the onboarding window is two weeks, mid-quarter arrives at week five, and finals are in week ten. Students who do not adjust their study cadence to match this structure consistently underperform relative to their preparation level.
