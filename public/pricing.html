Common questions

Is StudyEdge AI free?

Yes. The free plan includes 1 course, 2 AI tutor actions per day, and one free use of Coach Plan and Practice Exam, with no credit card. Pro and Unlimited unlock more course slots and remove daily caps.

Does the Pro free trial require a card?

Yes. The 7-day Pro trial runs through Stripe Checkout, which collects a card. Cancel any time during the trial to avoid being charged. The free plan has no card requirement.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Pro and Unlimited can be canceled at any time from your account or the Stripe customer portal. Cancellation stops future billing immediately.

What's the difference between Pro and Unlimited?

Pro gives you 5 courses and 100 AI actions per month. Unlimited removes the caps and adds AI Tutor with session memory plus advanced Practice Exam analytics including a predicted real-exam score. Unlimited does not include a free trial.

Why weekly billing?

Weekly billing is the lowest-commitment option, $2.99 a week for Pro. It maps to how students actually think about money and lets you stop or restart around exam seasons. Monthly and annual options are available with savings.
