Why memorizing terms is not studying sociology

Intro sociology students often memorize definitions and do fine. Upper-division sociology students who continue that approach fall behind, because the courses shift from testing recall to testing application and synthesis. StudyEdge AI is built for that shift, with sessions that practice the analytical skills from the beginning so upper-division courses do not catch you off guard.
