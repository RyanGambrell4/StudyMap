The short answer

The app does three things well: it builds your study schedule automatically, generates a detailed plan before every session, and runs the session with built-in AI tools. No tutors. No live classes. Just an AI system that treats studying the same way a coach would treat a training plan.

What StudyEdge AI actually does

There are three specific problems that StudyEdge AI solves:

1. Building the study schedule

Most students go into a semester without a real study plan. They know they have exams but do not have a structured week-by-week schedule that accounts for all of their courses, their workload, and the hours they can realistically sit down and study.

StudyEdge AI fixes this by taking your course list, your exam dates, and your available hours, and generating a full semester schedule automatically. It prioritizes the right courses at the right time and adjusts when things change.

2. Planning every session in advance

Even students with a schedule waste the first 20 minutes of every session figuring out what to actually work on. StudyEdge AI generates a Session Blueprint before each scheduled study block: a minute-by-minute plan that tells you exactly what topic to cover, in what sequence, and for how long. You open the app and start, no deciding required.

3. Running the session with active learning

Passive re-reading does not work. StudyEdge AI runs the session with active recall prompts, AI-generated flashcards, short quizzes, and an AI Tutor that can answer questions, explain concepts, and quiz you on your specific course material. All of this is built into the timer, so every session has structure and retrieval practice built in.

Other features

  • Grade Hub: Enter your assignment scores and exam weights. The app shows exactly what you need on each remaining assessment to hit your target grade.
  • AI Study Coach: A personalized multi-week study plan for your upcoming exams, not just a daily checklist.
  • AI Tutor: Ask questions about your course material. The AI answers with context, quizzes you back, and explains step by step.
  • Focus Mode: A built-in study timer that runs your Session Blueprint with structured breaks.
  • Streak tracking: Your study history and consistency over the semester.
