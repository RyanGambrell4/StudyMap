Common Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?
Open the app, go to Account → Manage Subscription. You can cancel anytime and keep access until the end of your billing period.
I was charged but can't access premium features.
Go to Account → Restore Purchases, or email us at support@getstudyedge.com and we'll fix it right away.
How do I delete my account?
Email support@getstudyedge.com with the subject "Delete My Account" and we'll process it within 48 hours.
The AI gave me an incorrect answer.
AI can make mistakes — always verify important information. If you notice a consistent issue, let us know so we can improve.
