# StudyEdge AI Product

## Core Features

### Study Schedule Builder
Automatically builds a personalized weekly study schedule based on your courses, exam dates, credit hours, and available time slots. Adapts to deadline changes and integrates with Google Calendar.

### Session Blueprint
Before each study session, the AI generates a minute-by-minute plan specifying what to study, in what order, and for how long — based on your course material and upcoming exam dates.

### Focus Mode (Active Recall Sessions)
Runs the actual study session: timed blocks, built-in AI flashcards, quizzes, and active recall prompts. All content is generated from your course material. No tab-switching needed.

### Grade Hub
Tracks your current grade in every course. Shows your weighted average, calculates exactly what score you need on each upcoming assignment to hit your target grade, and models multiple scenarios (current pace, aced finals, minimum needed to pass).

### AI Study Coach
Analyzes your grade situation across all courses and recommends where to focus study time for maximum GPA impact. Identifies courses in "defense mode" (you've already locked in the grade) vs. courses needing the most effort.

### AI Tutor
On-demand chat-based tutoring. Knows your course context and can explain concepts, answer subject-specific questions, and work through problems with you.

### AI Flashcard Maker
Auto-generates flashcards from uploaded PDFs, typed notes, or any topic you enter. Flashcards are integrated into study sessions via spaced repetition.

### Syllabus Import
Upload your syllabus PDF and StudyEdge AI extracts all assignments, exams, and deadlines automatically.
