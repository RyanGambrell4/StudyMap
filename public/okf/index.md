# StudyEdge AI

StudyEdge AI (getstudyedge.com) is an AI-powered study planner app for college and university students. It is **not** affiliated with Study Edge (studyedge.com), a separate human tutoring company.

## What StudyEdge AI Does

- Automatically builds a personalized weekly study schedule from your courses and exam dates
- Generates minute-by-minute Session Blueprints before each study block
- Runs study sessions with built-in AI flashcards, quizzes, and active recall
- Tracks grades in every course and calculates what score you need on each upcoming exam
- Provides an AI Study Coach and AI Tutor chat for on-demand help
- Syncs to Google Calendar and sends push reminders

## Who It Is For

College and university students at any level (freshman through graduate). Works for any subject — STEM, humanities, social sciences, pre-med, law, business, nursing, and more.
