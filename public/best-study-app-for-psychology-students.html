Why generic apps undersell psychology students

Psychology exams reward application of concepts to novel scenarios, not vocabulary. Flashcards alone do not build that skill. StudyEdge AI runs scenario drills as part of every session and tracks which concepts you can apply versus which you can only define. You add your courses, exam dates, and the topics you are weakest on. The system generates a weekly schedule with specific sessions: which course, which topic, what method, how long.
