Why reading the textbook is not enough in economics

Economics textbooks present clear models that seem logical when you are reading. Then the exam asks you to apply the model to an unfamiliar scenario and the logic disappears. The problem is that reading produces recognition, not retrieval. StudyEdge AI builds sessions around retrieval practice, forcing you to produce models, draw graphs, and solve problems from scratch.
