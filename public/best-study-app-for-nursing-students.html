How a typical nursing students week looks in StudyEdge AI

You add your courses, exam dates, and the topics you are weakest on. The system generates a weekly schedule with specific sessions: which course, which topic, what method, how long. Active recall runs in-session. After each session you log how confident you feel; the next week's schedule reweights toward shaky topics and lightens up on solid ones.
