Why Duke students need grade-tracking from day one

At Duke, the consequences of a poor semester early in undergrad can follow a student for years — especially on a pre-medical or pre-law track, where your cumulative GPA matters enormously for applications. Students who track their grade standing continuously are better positioned to make mid-semester adjustments than those who discover problems at finals. Knowing what you need on an exam before the exam is more useful than finding out afterward.
