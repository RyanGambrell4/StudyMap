Features

Not just a timer.
An entire study system.

Focus Mode

Every session is broken into structured blocks — warm-up, deep work, active recall, and synthesis. StudyEdge keeps the clock and tells you exactly what to do in each block.

Session Blueprint

Before every session, the AI builds a personalized study plan based on your course, your goals, and how much time you have until your exam. It's like having a tutor map out exactly how to spend the next hour.

Streak Tracking

Consistency is the difference between cramming and actually learning. Your streak makes showing up feel like winning.

Study Tools Built In

During any session, access AI-generated flashcards, multiple choice quizzes, active recall prompts, and a notes area. Everything you need to learn the material — not just read it.
