How StudyEdge works: Three problems every student has. One system that fixes all of them.

1. Your study schedule, built automatically around your real life

Add your classes and due dates. StudyEdge maps out your entire week automatically, filling your free time with the right courses at the right time, prioritized by exam date and difficulty. Push reminders handle the rest.

2. Every study session planned before you open a book

Before every study session, StudyEdge AI builds a minute-by-minute Session Blueprint for your specific course, upcoming exams, and goals. Warm-up, deep review, active recall, synthesis — all mapped out before you sit down.

3. An AI study coach that runs the session while you're in it

Hit start and StudyEdge runs the clock. Each block is timed and structured. Flashcards, recall prompts, practice quizzes, and notes are built in. You're working the material, not reading over slides hoping it sticks.

Frequently Asked Questions about StudyEdge

What is StudyEdge?

StudyEdge is an AI-powered study planner for college and university students. It automatically builds your study schedule around your classes and deadlines, plans every study session minute-by-minute, and coaches you through it with flashcards, quizzes, and active recall built in.

Is StudyEdge free?

Yes. StudyEdge has a free plan with one course, basic calendar, Focus Mode timer, and 10 AI study boosts per month. No credit card required. Pro plans start at $12.99/month.

Does StudyEdge work for any major?

Yes. StudyEdge works for any subject at the college or university level including STEM, humanities, and social sciences. You upload your syllabus, add your courses, and the AI adapts its Session Blueprints to your material and exam timeline.

How is StudyEdge different from Notion, Anki, or Quizlet?

Notion is a blank document; Anki is only flashcards; Quizlet is only quizzes. StudyEdge is the complete study system: it builds the schedule, plans every session, runs the timer, and has flashcards, quizzes, active recall prompts, an AI tutor, and grade tracking all stitched into a single focus session. No tab-switching, no app-hopping.
