## Voice fingerprint
- Funny vs serious: 0.2 (lean serious — students are stressed about grades)
- Formal vs casual: 0.35 (casual but not slangy; like a smart older student)
- Respectful vs irreverent: 0.3 (honest and direct; calls out bad habits without preaching)
- Enthusiastic vs matter-of-fact: 0.35 (let research do the persuading)
